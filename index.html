Welcome to ACC

Asian Computer College (ACC) is a premier non-sectarian private institution located in the heart of Mayapa, Calamba City, Laguna. Our state-of-the-art campus provides a safe, nurturing, and technologically advanced environment where students flourish academically, develop leadership skills, and master cutting-edge technology.

We believe in nurturing not just brilliant minds, but also strong character. At ACC, every learner is empowered to dream big, think critically, and become a positive changemaker in our rapidly evolving digital world.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To advance knowledge, cultivate potentials and create success through outcomes-based education anchored on local and global standards.

🌟

Our Vision

Asian Computer College and ACC Institute endeavor to become exemplars of excellence in standards-based and outcome-based education in the City of Calamba, Laguna that develop and train a new breed of individuals who are globally competitive and holistically prepared to take on the challenges of the 21st century.

Our Rich History

📚

Founded in 2000, Asian Computer College has been at the forefront of educational innovation for over two decades. We continue to evolve and grow, developing programs specifically designed for the digital generation while maintaining our commitment to traditional values and academic excellence.
